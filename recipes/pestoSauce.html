PESTO SAUCE

  • Pine Nuts: 1 cup
  • Garlic: 2 TBS
  • Basil: 4 cups
  • Parmesan Cheese (grated): 1 cup
  • White Wine: 1/3 cup
  • *Lemon Juice: 1/3 cup
  • Chicken Broth (less/reduced sodium): .5 cup
  • Salt: .5 tsp

Toast pine nuts. Puree basil, pine nuts, and garlic. Add parmesan cheese, wine, lemon juice and chicken and blend adding salt o taste. Serve on chicken, pasta, or seafood. One serving is 2 TBS.

Number of Servings: 20
